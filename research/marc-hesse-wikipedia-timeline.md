## Research Areas and Contributions

### Geological CO2 Storage and Natural CO2 Reservoirs
**Focus:** Dynamics of CO2 dissolution in saline aquifers; natural CO2 reservoirs as analogues for geological carbon storage
**Key Study:** Bravo Dome natural CO2 reservoir (New Mexico) — estimated 366 MtCO2 (22% of original 1.6 GtCO2) dissolved into brine over 1.2–1.4 Ma
**Key Publications:** Sathaye et al. (2014, 2016a, 2016b); Akhbari & Hesse (2016); Wen et al. (2018)
**DOE Feature:** Research featured on the front page of the U.S. Department of Energy website[^5]
**Reference:** JSG research page[^6]; Oden Institute news[^5]

### Dynamics of Partially Molten Materials
**Focus:** Partial melting and melt segregation in planetary interiors; texturally equilibrated pore networks; viscous compaction of solid matrix
**Key Publications:** Ghanbarzadeh et al. (2014, 2015a, 2015b, 2017); Jordan & Hesse (2015); Jordan et al. (2018)
**Reference:** JSG research page[^6]

### Reactive Transport in Porous Media
**Focus:** Chemical wave theory; hyperbolic PDE systems for multi-component reactive transport; pH-dependent sorption; noble gas partitioning
**Key Publications:** Venkatraman et al. (2014); McNeece & Hesse (2016, 2017, 2018); Zhang et al. (2017, 2018a, 2018b)
**Reference:** JSG research page[^6]

### Geomechanical Inverse Problems
**Focus:** Satellite geodesy and GPS data integration into poroelastic models; subsurface flow-deformation coupling; induced seismicity
**Key Publications:** Hesse & Stadler (2014); McCormack & Hesse (2018, 2020); Alghamdi et al. (2020)
**Reference:** JSG research page[^6]

### Habitability of Icy Ocean Worlds
**Focus:** Hydrothermal evolution of icy moons (Europa, Enceladus); primordial planetesimal evolution; ice-penetrating radar interpretation
**Key Publications:** Ren et al. (2022, 2024a, 2024b); Wolfenbarger et al. (2021); Carnahan et al. (2021, 2022a, 2022b)
**Reference:** Oden Institute biography[^1]; JSG people page[^7]
