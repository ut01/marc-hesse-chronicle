### Education / Research

### Academic Position
- Professor in the Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biophysics at Columbia University, New York.
- Member of the Herbert Irving Comprehensive Cancer Center (HICCC).
- Principal Investigator of the Greene Lab at Columbia University Medical Center.

### Research Contributions / Innovations
Pioneering work in single-molecule imaging and DNA repair mechanisms:
- Developed the "DNA curtains" technique - a novel high-throughput single-molecule visualization method combining total internal reflection fluorescence microscopy, lipid fluidity, microfluidics, and nano-fabrication
- Groundbreaking research on CRISPR-Cas9 DNA interrogation mechanisms (highly cited work with 1100+ citations)
- Studies on homologous recombination and RAD51 recombinase family proteins
- Research on DNA repair pathways and genome stability
- Investigations into base triplet stepping mechanisms during DNA recombination
